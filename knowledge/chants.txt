I worship Govinda, the primeval Lord, Krishna, who danced the rasa dance in the land of Vrindavan; who is always surrounded by the gopis, who destroyed the pride of the demoness Putana, and whose form is the life and soul of all incarnations.
I worship Govinda, the primeval Lord, who nourishes all the demigods and beings,band who, with his flute, entrances the whole universe.
